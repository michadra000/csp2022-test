Welcome to Overwatch

Overwatch is the best game in my opinion because of how it is set up, it is a 5v5 online battle game where you play as either a tank, a support, or a damage character and those different catagories of characters have their own special abilities and attributes like tanks for example have way more health then a healer and they are ment to take all the heavy damage while the Support characters dont have much health but they can heal other players with either magic or guns, damage on the other hand is a clear mixture of both, damage do the most damage but dont have the most health and dont have the best healing abilities but instead they have the best damage dealing attacks and are the best to fight people with. Overwatch has many modes and different characters to discover and I hope you will try it out for yourself. The different role's in Overwatch do different things to suport their team, Tanks hero's role is to take the most damage and protect the other players by keeping the damage on them while creating things like barriers and shields with their abilities to assist them in their team. Damage hero's role is to do the most damage possible for their team to help kill the other teams hero's, they often have abilities that help them do more damage or transportation so they get to the fight quicker. Support hero's role is to help everybody on the team stay alive, they provide healing and even some boosts to character damage and speed so they can fight with even more damage and health.
